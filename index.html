About Lena

A believer in
slow looking
and honest light.

Based in Berlin and working across Europe, I move between portraiture and editorial — the space where a brief becomes a conversation, and a conversation becomes a photograph that outlasts both.

My process is built on three things: patience, natural light, and the quiet moment just before a subject remembers they're being photographed. That's where I work.

Before I raise the camera I want to understand the person in front of it — what they're afraid of, what makes them laugh without thinking. The real image lives in that knowledge.

The Philosophy

Stillness is not
the absence of
movement.

In a world optimised for distraction, the portrait is an act of resistance. To sit with someone long enough that the performance dissolves — that is the work. Not capture. Presence.

I shoot primarily with available light, often in the early morning or the hour before sunset. Not for aesthetic reasons alone, but because that light asks something of both the photographer and the subject: attention. Slowness. Being exactly where you are.

Stille Kraft series, Berlin 2022
On Collaboration

I work with you.
Not at you.

Every subject brings something I have not photographed before: a particular quality of attention, a held posture, a smile that takes twenty minutes to arrive and then changes everything. My job is to be ready for it — and to create the conditions in which it can happen.

I limit myself to two commissions per month. Not as a marketing device, but because making something worth keeping takes time.

How I Work

The shape
of a session

1

Conversation

We speak before anything else — a call, a coffee, a long walk through Mitte. I want to understand the person before I understand the brief. This is not optional.

2

Visual Research

References, light studies, location visits. I read the available light at the planned location across several mornings before we shoot a single frame.

3

The Shoot

Unhurried. I prefer long sessions that allow the subject to forget the camera exists. The best frames arrive in the quiet between intentions.

4

Delivery

Colour-graded, retouched files delivered within ten working days. One round of creative review. No watermarks, no subscriptions — your images, cleanly.
